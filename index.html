Material Design

Material is a design system created by Google to help teams build high-quality digital experiences for Android, iOS, Flutter, and the web. Material Design is inspired by the physical world and its textures, including how they reflect light and cast shadows.

Foundation

Material Design defines the qualities that can be expressed by UI regions, surfaces, and components. Design and strategize how your app is built using foundations that address design from both a broad and detailed perspective.

The color system

The Material Design color system helps you apply color to your UI in a meaningful way. In this system, you select a primary and a secondary color to represent your brand. Dark and light variants of each color can then be applied to your UI in different ways.
